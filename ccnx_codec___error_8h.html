size_t ccnxCodecError_GetByteOffset ( const CCNxCodecError *  error)

The byte offset of the error.

Primarily for decoding errors. It will contain the byte offset of the first byte of the field causing the error. For encoding, it will be the byte offer of the partially-encoded buffer, but the error is usually in the native format, not the partially encoded buffer.
